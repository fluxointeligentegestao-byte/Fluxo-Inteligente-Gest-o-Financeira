# Project Instructions

## Financial Structure Rules
- **DO NOT** modify the standard Chart of Accounts (`UNIVERSAL_CHART_OF_ACCOUNTS`) in `src/constants/financial.ts` unless explicitly requested by the user.
- **DO NOT** modify the standard Cost Centers (`UNIVERSAL_COST_CENTERS`) in `src/constants/financial.ts` unless explicitly requested by the user.
- These structures must remain detailed and consistent across all clients.
- The Chart of Accounts must follow the 3-level hierarchy: Group (N1), Category (N2), and Detailed Description (N3).
